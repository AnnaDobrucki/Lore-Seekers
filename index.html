What is Lore Seekers?

We are a workshop based comapny that strives towards helping people understand the, sometimes overwhelming, world of Dungeons & Dragons. We are here to lend a helping hand to anyone curious and brave enough to come to one of our 2 hour workshops. Take a look at what we have to offer and join in here!
